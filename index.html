About Me

I have a B.Sc. in Computer Engineering and Information Systems acquired at Tecnocampus Mataró (Universitat Pompeu Fabra, Barcelona), and I recently graduated with distinction with a Masters in Computer Science, specialized in AR/VR at Trinity College Dublin. 

My main strengths lie in problem solving and the ability to quickly learn and implement new technologies. On the professional field, I have worked with companies of varying sizes building production-ready Full-stacks WebApps with ML implementations (age and gender recognition) from the ground up. I have also done beginner level Python and Machine Learning courses.

I love learning about new technologies and keeping track of the latest trends in AR/VR and ML. During my Masters thesis, I developed 3D Copy&Paste, a 3D content creation solution that combines salient object detection and Photogrammetry to improve 3D scanning of objects on mobile devices. I have listed both my personal and professional projects on my portfolio.
